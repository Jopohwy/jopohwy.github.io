I'm Joshua, a recent graduate from LSE's MSc Global Management programme with a First Class Honours in Accounting and Finance from Bayes Business School. My background spans M&A financial due diligence at Deloitte, spatial analytics at CACI, and three years of equity research within a student-run live investment fund.

What draws me to the intersection of finance and strategy is the same thing that drew me to launch my own distribution business: the belief that sharp analysis and clear thinking should lead to something real. I'm comfortable with ambiguity, energised by complex problems, and genuinely curious about how businesses create and sustain competitive advantage.

Beyond the professional, I am currently completing the SUTD Certified Generative AI Professional programme, and I hold a Deloitte Amplify GenAI Business Case Challenge first-place win — presented to a Partner and C-suite panel on AI transformation in hospitality.
